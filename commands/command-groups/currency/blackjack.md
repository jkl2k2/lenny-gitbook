# blackjack

## Aliases: `bj`

## Usage

1. `!blackjack [bet]`
   1. **"hit" / "h"** = grab another card
   2. **"stay" / "stand" / "s"** = lock in your current hand
   3. **"double" / "d"** = double your bet and hit once

## Cooldown: `3s`
